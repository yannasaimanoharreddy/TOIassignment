Think of it Foundation (TOFI)

Connecting youth for thought leadership via TOFI-Youth Leadership Council By the Youth | For the Youth

ABOUT:

Think of It Foundation is a non profit youth-led like-minded community for young leaders to discover themselves via thought-leadership and meaningful nation building activities through TOFI - Youth Leadership Council. Working with young minds to create societal change, awareness about contemporary issues and policies and build life-long relations, TOFI - YLC aims to revolutionize traditional leadership definition and create leaders with TOFI impact model and regulatory council.
